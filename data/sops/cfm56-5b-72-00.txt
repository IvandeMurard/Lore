STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP Reference: 72-00-00
Engine: CFM56-5B Series
Subject: Fan Section General Inspection Procedure

1. SCOPE
This procedure covers the routine visual and manual inspection of the CFM56-5B fan section during scheduled maintenance checks.

2. INSPECTION FREQUENCY
- A-Check: Visual inspection of accessible fan blades and spinner.
- C-Check: Full fan section inspection including borescope of fan disk.
- Engine change: Complete fan module inspection per AMM 72-00-00-800.

3. VISUAL INSPECTION PROCEDURE

3.1 Fan Blades
- Inspect all fan blades for nicks, dents, erosion, and foreign object damage.
- Nicks up to 2mm depth on leading edge: ACCEPTABLE — blend per AMM 72-21-01-100.
- Nicks 2mm to 4mm: ENGINEERING REVIEW required before continued operation.
- Nicks above 4mm or cracks of any size: BLADE REPLACEMENT required.

3.2 Fan Spinner
- Check spinner for cracks, dents, and secure attachment.
- Verify all spinner screws are present and torqued.

3.3 Fan Containment Case
- Inspect for rubbing indicators, oil leaks, and structural damage.
- Any evidence of blade tip rubbing requires escalation to engineering.

4. BLADE EROSION MONITORING

4.1 Leading edge erosion is expected on high-cycle engines.
4.2 Record erosion measurements at each C-Check and compare to limits in AMM 72-21-01-300.
4.3 Erosion within limits: continue monitoring. Flag if rate exceeds 0.1mm per 1000 cycles.

5. DOCUMENTATION
All inspection findings must be recorded in the engine logbook with reference to this SOP. Photographs are recommended for any findings above routine.
